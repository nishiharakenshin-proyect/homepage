02 · Our story

Born in the
heart of the laneway.

27 Degraves · Melbourne CBD
★Best
Café VIC

In 2017 we squeezed a hand-built espresso bar into 40 square metres of Degraves Lane. Our first cup went to a stranger who became a regular that afternoon. Melbourne's laneway culture — spontaneous, creative, unhurried — has shaped everything we do.

We source direct from smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasting in small batches each week. Coffee is our craft; community is our purpose.
