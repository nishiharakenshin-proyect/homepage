Frequently asked

How far in advance should I book? +
Signature cuts book 1–2 weeks ahead. Colour and balayage are typically 3–4 weeks. Bridal parties, please reach out 6+ months in advance.
What's your cancellation policy? +
Free changes with 48 hours notice. Inside 48 hours a 50% fee applies to cover our team's time. We'll always work with you if something unexpected comes up.
Do you take first-time guests? +
Yes, and we love meeting new clients. Choose "First-Visit Ritual" when booking — it includes an extended consultation and our gentlest approach.
Are your products truly organic? +
Every product on our shelf is ACO-certified or equivalent. Our colour is 100% ammonia-free; our smoothing treatment is formaldehyde-free. We publish the full list on request.
Gift cards & packages? +
Digital gift cards are available from $50–$500 and can be emailed instantly. Multi-visit ritual packages give you ~15% off when prepaid for three visits.
