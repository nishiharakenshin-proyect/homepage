§ 06 — The Chef

Thomas Rourke.

"The ember does the cooking. My job is to know when to let it go."

Trained at Asador Etxebarri in the Basque hills, Thomas returned to Melbourne in 2017 with a single obsession: the quiet discipline of wood-fire cooking. He has not changed his apron since.
