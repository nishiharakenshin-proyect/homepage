Our Story

Born in the
Heart of the Laneway

In 2017, we squeezed a hand-built espresso bar into 40 square metres of Degraves Lane and made our first cup for a stranger who became a regular that afternoon. Melbourne's laneway culture — spontaneous, creative, unhurried — has shaped everything we do.

We source directly from smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasting in small batches each week to honour every note of flavour nature intended. Coffee is our craft, but community is our purpose.
