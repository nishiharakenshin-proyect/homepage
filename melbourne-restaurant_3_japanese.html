§ 04 — The Chef

Haruki Tanabe.

田 辺 晴 樹
Shōjin is not about what you remove. It is about what you notice once the noise is gone.

Trained at Kōdai-ji temple in Kyoto, later at Narisawa and Kikunoi. At AOI, he cooks alone — one stove, one board, one breath.
