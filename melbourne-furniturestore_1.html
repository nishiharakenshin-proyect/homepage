§ 01 — The Timber

Only wood that has already lived.

Every board is salvaged — storm-fall, thinning, and reclaimed beams from Victorian shearing sheds. Nothing is cut down to make what we make.

§ 02 — The Silence
"We do not use machines louder than the birds. When the birds stop, we stop."
— Marcus Hale, Founder
§ 03 — The Workshop

A single cabin under the skylight.

Tallow-coated floor, two benches, and an anvil that has been used for sixteen years.

This is where the furniture is made.
