Our story
ABOUT US

Born in a laneway.
Raised on sugar.

GLAZED. started in 2019 with a pop-up stand in a Fitzroy laneway — just one fryer, a dream, and a recipe passed down three generations. We believed Melbourne deserved donuts that were more than a sugar hit — they should be an experience.

Every donut is handcrafted from scratch daily using organic flour, free-range eggs, and glazes made in-house. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just good dough, great coffee, and a space where you can slow down.
