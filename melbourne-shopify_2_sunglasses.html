04 — Our Story

Seven years of
chasing light.

LUMEN was founded in 2019 by two friends who shared an obsession with the way light bends through glass. We started with a single frame cut by hand in a Florence workshop — we're still cutting every frame there today.

Every pair of LUMEN sunglasses passes through twelve sets of hands before it reaches yours. No shortcuts, no machine polishing, no compromises on the acetate. Just slow, careful work.
