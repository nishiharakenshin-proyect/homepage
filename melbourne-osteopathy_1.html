02 · Our method

Slow down,
then rebuild.

STEP 01 · 15 MIN
Listen
We start with the story — injury history, work habits, sleep, sport. Before hands, questions.
STEP 02 · 20 MIN
Assess
Functional movement screen, joint-by-joint range testing, neurological screen where indicated.
STEP 03 · 25 MIN
Treat
Hands-on osteopathic technique selected to your presentation — articulation, soft tissue, gentle HVLA where appropriate.
STEP 04 · ONGOING
Rebuild
A short, specific home program. We review and reload each visit — no open-ended dependence on the clinic.
